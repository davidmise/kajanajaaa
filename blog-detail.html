Embedding scent, sound, and typography inside premium hospitality.

Design notes from our Serengeti Reserve residency and the multi-sensory playbook it inspired.

Every Kajnaja hospitality project begins with field recordings. We capture the hush before sunrise, the cadence of Kiswahili greetings, and the metallic clink of flatware. These sounds guide the tempo of our typography and motion rules. It ensures the identity breathes like the property.

For Serengeti Reserve we paired a custom serif rooted in coastal Swahili calligraphy with a modern grotesk. The palette shifts from deep ocean to salmon dawn. Scent cues were developed with perfumers incorporating baobab, sandalwood, and sea spray.

“Invisible luxury is when the brand whispers through all five senses.”

We then choreographed touchpoints: projection-mapped tasting rooms, brass keycards, and a digital concierge that mirrors the lobby lighting. The Continuity Lab monitors guest sentiment weekly and updates the system in real-time.
