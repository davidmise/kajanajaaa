About

We are the calm custodians of Tanzania’s most ambitious brands.

Founded by Najma Kajanja in Masaki, our studio brings together anthropologists, art directors, motion designers, and scent artists. We blend couture craft with sharp commercial rigor to make brands that feel inevitable.

Who we are

Company overview, mission, and values anchored in Tanzania.

Company Overview

Kajnaja Brand is headquartered in Dar es Salaam with satellite collaborators across Zanzibar, Nairobi, and Kigali. We orchestrate physical and digital touchpoints from one calm hub.

Mission

Craft calm, luxurious African brands that scale without losing their soul.

Vision

Become the blueprint for ocean-blue elegance across the continent.

Values

  • Precision over pace
  • Context before concept
  • People-first collaboration
  • Invisible luxury
